3 — How Segments Connect to the Workflow
1. Create Organization Admin picks a segment: "This org is Retail." The segment is stored on the organization record.
2. Onboard a Site In the Modules step, the segment's suggested modules are pre-selected: Cold Chain ✓, Energy ✓, Safety ✓. Admin can add/remove before activating.
3. Activate Modules Each activated module creates alert rules from its recipes for matching devices. The segment just pre-selects — it doesn't force anything.
No sensor field needed Segments don't define sensors directly. Sensors come from the modules → each module has required_sensor_models. When you pick modules, the sensors are implied.

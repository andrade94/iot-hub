📐 Design Notes

  • Floor plan is primary — Full-width spatial view with interactive device dots. Operators think spatially, not in lists.
  • Zone summary strip — Compact pills below the floor plan. Each shows: zone name, device count, current temp, range, health bar. Click → navigates to zone detail.
  • Fallback — When no floor plans exist, show the zone cards grid (current behavior) + dashed CTA to upload.
  • Alert filters — Search + severity segmented control. Same pattern as Assets tab device filters.
  • Section divider says "Plano del Sitio" not "Zonas" — because the floor plan IS the zones view, just spatial.
  • Zone pills are clickable — each navigates to /sites/{id}/zones/{zone}.
  • Device dots on floor plan — green=online, red=offline. Hover shows tooltip with name + current reading.

02 / 08
The Problem

Manual Monitoring is Broken

Businesses with temperature-sensitive assets, energy infrastructure, and compliance obligations rely on manual checks, paper logs, and reactive responses. The result: product loss, regulatory fines, and operational blind spots.

🔢

Manual Temperature Logs

Staff record temperatures 2-3x daily on paper. Gaps go unnoticed. Overnight excursions destroy inventory.

🔓

Compliance Blind Spots

NOM-251, COFEPRIS, and HACCP audits require continuous records. Paper logs have gaps, errors, and no timestamps.

⏱

Slow Response Times

Equipment failures at night go undetected until morning. Hours of exposure to unsafe temperatures. Costly waste.

📈

No Operational Visibility

Regional managers have no real-time view across sites. Performance data is siloed in spreadsheets.

03 / 08
The Solution

Astrea IoT Platform

A unified operations platform that connects LoRaWAN sensors to real-time dashboards, automated alerts, and compliance-ready reports. Three interfaces serve every level of the organization:

📱

WhatsApp

Critical alerts delivered instantly. One-tap acknowledgment. Escalation chains ensure no alert is ignored.

📷

Mobile App

Daily monitoring on the go. Push notifications, device status, work orders, and field documentation.

💻

Web Platform

Full configuration, deep analytics, compliance reports, user management, and multi-site command center.

04 / 08
Key Features

Six Segment Modules

Activate modules per client based on their industry. Each module includes dashboards, alert recipes, and compliance tools tailored to the vertical.

❄

Cold Chain

Temperature zones, defrost detection, HACCP reports, NOM-251 compliance.

⚡

Energy

Compressor duty cycles, night waste detection, energy cost optimization.

📋

Compliance

Calendar, reminders, corrective actions, LFPDPPP consent, audit exports.

⚙

Industrial

Vibration, compressed air, equipment utilization, predictive maintenance.

🍃

IAQ

CO2, PM2.5, humidity monitoring, zone scoring, HVAC correlation.

🚪

Safety & People

Door monitoring, traffic counting, occupancy heatmaps, access events.

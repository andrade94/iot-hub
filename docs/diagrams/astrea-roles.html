Roles and Permissions

7 roles across Astrea (internal) and client organizations, with 29 granular permissions.

Role Definitions

super_admin Astrea
Full platform access. All organizations, all sites. Command Center access. Can create/delete organizations, manage billing, and configure the entire platform.
Scope: All sites, all organizations
support Astrea
Astrea support staff. Read access to all organizations for troubleshooting. Can view devices, alerts, and logs. Cannot modify billing or org settings.
Scope: All sites (read), all organizations (read)
account_manager Astrea
Manages client relationships. Can view and manage billing, subscriptions, and org-level configuration for assigned client accounts.
Scope: Assigned organizations, all sites within
technician Astrea
Field technician. Device diagnostics, work orders, battery and signal info. Can complete work orders, upload photos, and manage device replacements.
Scope: Assigned sites (via user_sites)
client_org_admin Client
Client organization administrator. Full access to their org: all sites, billing, user management, alert configuration, reports, and settings.
Scope: All sites in their organization
client_site_manager Client
Regional manager. Monitors multiple assigned sites. Dashboards, alerts, reports, site comparison. Can acknowledge alerts and manage work orders.
Scope: Multiple assigned sites (via user_sites)
client_site_viewer Client
Store manager. Read-only access to a single site. Can view dashboards and reports, acknowledge alerts, and request maintenance.
Scope: Single assigned site

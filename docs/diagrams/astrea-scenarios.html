Organization Admin

client_org_admin -- Director de Operaciones
A Day in the Life

Maria is the Director of Operations for a convenience store chain with 45 locations. She starts her morning reviewing the corporate summary email that arrived at 8:00 AM. Three sites had temperature excursions overnight -- she drills into the dashboard to check if corrective actions were logged. She notices one site has had recurring compressor issues and creates a work order for the technician team. Before her 10 AM meeting, she pulls a weekly compliance report PDF to share with her board. In the afternoon, she onboards two new sites using the wizard, assigns regional managers, and adjusts alert thresholds for the pharmacy locations.

Typical Daily Timeline

08:00 Reviews corporate summary email with overnight alerts across all 45 sites
08:30 Drills into dashboard for sites with temperature excursions, checks corrective actions
09:00 Creates work orders for recurring equipment issues, assigns to technicians
09:45 Generates weekly compliance PDF report for board meeting
14:00 Onboards 2 new sites via the setup wizard (gateway, devices, modules, escalation)
15:00 Manages users: assigns regional manager to new sites, adjusts alert rules for pharmacy
16:30 Reviews billing invoices and subscription details for the month

Key Workflows

  • Site onboarding wizard (WF-023)
  • User management and role assignment (WF-009)
  • Report generation and scheduling (WF-006, WF-022)
  • Alert rule configuration and recipes (WF-011)
  • Billing and invoicing review (WF-007)
  • Compliance calendar management (WF-008)
  • Data export for integration (WF-015)

Most Used Pages

  • /dashboard -- Site overview with KPI cards
  • /settings/users -- User management
  • /settings/sites -- Site configuration
  • /reports -- Report generation
  • /settings/billing -- Subscription and invoices
  • /settings/alert-rules -- Alert rule management
  • /analytics/alerts -- Alert trends and analytics

Alerts Received

Critical: Escalation only (when site_manager and site_viewer do not respond)
Weekly corporate summary email digest
Compliance reminders at 30, 7, and 1 day before due dates

Site Manager

client_site_manager -- Gerente Regional
A Day in the Life

Carlos manages 12 stores in the northern region. His morning starts with the regional summary push notification at 7:30 AM -- 30 minutes after the earliest site opens. He scans the mobile app while commuting: two sites have high-severity alerts from overnight. He acknowledges one (a brief temp spike during a known defrost cycle) and escalates the other to the on-site viewer for immediate inspection. At the office, he opens the web platform to compare performance across his 12 sites, identifying two consistently underperforming locations. He schedules a meeting with those site viewers and generates a comparison report. In the afternoon, he reviews work order completion rates and assigns a technician for a battery replacement at a remote site.

Typical Daily Timeline

07:30 Receives regional summary push notification on mobile app
08:00 Reviews alerts on mobile during commute, acknowledges or escalates
09:00 Opens web dashboard, compares performance across 12 sites
10:00 Generates comparison report for underperforming sites
14:00 Reviews work order completion rates and technician assignments
15:00 Checks device health across sites, flags low-battery sensors
16:00 Creates maintenance windows for scheduled equipment service

Key Workflows

  • Alert acknowledgment and escalation (WF-003)
  • Work order creation and assignment (WF-004)
  • Report generation (WF-006)
  • Maintenance window management (WF-016)
  • Device replacement initiation (WF-014)
  • Corrective action logging (WF-013)
  • Site template capture for cloning (WF-018)

Most Used Pages

  • /dashboard -- Multi-site overview
  • /alerts -- Alert list with filters
  • /work-orders -- Work order management
  • /reports -- Report generation
  • /devices -- Device health and status
  • /analytics/alerts -- Alert analytics
  • /settings/maintenance -- Maintenance windows

Alerts Received

Critical alerts: immediate WhatsApp escalation
High alerts: push notification + email
Regional morning summary (daily push + email)
Device offline and battery warnings via push

Site Viewer

client_site_viewer -- Gerente de Tienda
A Day in the Life

Ana manages a single convenience store. She arrives at 7:00 AM and checks the morning summary that arrived on WhatsApp -- overnight her walk-in cooler had a brief temperature excursion that auto-resolved. She opens the mobile app to verify current readings: all zones are green. Mid-morning, she receives a push notification that the front display case temperature is rising. She walks over, finds a customer left the door ajar, closes it, and acknowledges the alert on her phone. Before closing, she reviews the daily temperature log on the dashboard to ensure compliance for a COFEPRIS visit next week. She notices the freezer sensor battery is at 18% and taps "Request Maintenance" to notify the technician team.

Typical Daily Timeline

07:00 Checks WhatsApp morning summary -- reviews overnight activity at her store
07:15 Opens mobile app to verify all temperature zones are in range
10:30 Receives push alert about rising temperature in display case
10:35 Investigates, finds root cause (door ajar), acknowledges alert
17:00 Reviews daily temperature log on dashboard for compliance
17:15 Notices low battery sensor, taps "Request Maintenance" to notify technician

Key Workflows

  • Alert acknowledgment on mobile (WF-003)
  • Corrective action logging (WF-013)
  • Morning summary review (WF-005)
  • Report viewing (WF-006)
  • Maintenance request (WF-004)

Most Used Pages

  • /dashboard -- Single-site overview
  • /alerts -- Alert list for their store
  • /devices -- Device status (read-only)
  • /reports -- View and download reports
  • Mobile app -- Primary daily interface

Alerts Received

Critical + high alerts: immediate WhatsApp + push
All alerts for their store: push notification
Morning summary via WhatsApp (daily at store opening hour)

Technician

technician -- Tecnico de Mantenimiento
A Day in the Life

Ricardo is a field technician responsible for 30 sites across the metro area. His day starts by checking the mobile app for new work orders -- there are three: a battery replacement at a pharmacy, a sensor replacement at a convenience store (the device was reporting erratic readings), and a gateway inspection at an industrial site. He drives to the first location, opens the work order detail, takes a photo of the old sensor, replaces the battery, uploads a completion photo, and marks the work order complete. At the second site, he uses the device replacement flow to swap the faulty sensor -- the system transfers alert rules to the new device automatically. Between visits, he checks the device health dashboard to proactively identify sensors that might need attention soon.

Typical Daily Timeline

07:30 Checks mobile app for new work order assignments (3 pending today)
08:30 Arrives at pharmacy, opens work order, photographs old sensor
09:00 Replaces battery, uploads completion photo, marks work order complete
10:30 At convenience store, uses device replacement flow to swap faulty sensor
11:00 Verifies new sensor is reporting correctly via real-time dashboard
13:00 Checks device health dashboard for proactive maintenance needs
14:00 Gateway inspection at industrial site, checks signal strength and connectivity
16:00 Reviews completed work orders, adds notes for follow-up items

Key Workflows

  • Work order execution and completion (WF-004)
  • Device replacement with rule transfer (WF-014)
  • Device health monitoring (WF-025)
  • Photo documentation for work orders
  • Gateway diagnostics and inspection

Most Used Pages

  • /work-orders -- Assignment list
  • /devices -- Device status and diagnostics
  • /devices/{id} -- Device detail (signal, battery, readings)
  • /gateways -- Gateway health and connectivity
  • Mobile app -- Primary field interface

Alerts Received

Device offline alerts: WhatsApp + push
Low battery warnings: push notification
New work order assignments: push notification
Device health anomalies: in-app notification

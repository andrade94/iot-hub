Glossary

Alert
A notification generated when a sensor reading breaches a configured threshold for a sustained duration. Alerts have severity levels (critical, high, medium, low) and follow a lifecycle: active, acknowledged, resolved, or dismissed.

A configuration that defines when an alert should fire. Includes threshold value, condition (above/below), duration (how long the breach must persist), severity, and cooldown period. Three types: simple, correlation, and baseline.

The service that determines how to deliver alert notifications. Routes by severity: critical/high get immediate escalation, medium/low follow normal queue. Implements batch mode when more than 5 alerts fire in 10 minutes.

Segment Modules

Activate modules per client organization. Each module brings purpose-built dashboards, alert recipes, and compliance tools.

❄

Cold Chain

Temperature monitoring for retail, convenience stores, pharmacies, and foodservice operations.

  • Temperature zone grouping (walk-in, vitrinas, freezer)
  • Defrost cycle auto-detection (48h learning)
  • HACCP report generation with excursion flagging
  • Cold chain timeline with 24h/7d/30d views
  • NOM-251 compliance documentation
⚡

Energy

Current and power monitoring for cost optimization and equipment health tracking.

  • Compressor duty cycle analytics (CT101 sensors)
  • Night waste detection (22:00-06:00 consumption)
  • Energy cost reports with peak/off-peak breakdown
  • Equipment degradation early warning
📋

Compliance

Automated compliance event tracking with reminders, corrective actions, and audit-ready exports.

  • Compliance calendar with automated reminders
  • Corrective action logging on alerts
  • LFPDPPP (Mexican privacy law) consent management
  • Activity log audit trail
⚙

Industrial

Vibration, compressed air, and environmental monitoring for manufacturing and industrial facilities.

  • Vibration pattern analysis
  • Compressed air leak detection
  • Equipment utilization dashboards
  • Predictive maintenance triggers
🍃

IAQ (Indoor Air Quality)

CO2, PM2.5, humidity, and temperature monitoring for offices, schools, and commercial real estate.

  • Composite air quality zone scoring (AM307)
  • CO2 concentration tracking and alerts
  • Occupancy-based HVAC correlation
  • Indoor comfort index dashboards
🚪

Safety & People

Door access monitoring, occupancy counting, and traffic flow analytics for retail and commercial spaces.

  • Door open/close pattern analytics (WS301)
  • Customer traffic snapshots (VS121)
  • Occupancy heatmaps per zone
  • Access event timeline
